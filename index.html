Detailed Scope of Services

📊 Direct Tax Services

+

Annual Income Tax (ITR) Filing

  • Prepare Annual Computation of Income and Tax Statement
  • Compute taxable income and tax payable
  • Collate and review necessary documents for filing
  • Assist with preparation and e-filing of the ITR
  • Support with e-verification

Monthly TDS Payment & Quarterly Returns

  • Calculate monthly TDS liabilities for salaries/contractors
  • Generate challans and provide challan details
  • Importation/verification of challans from IT portal
  • E-verification of PANs via TRACES
  • Preparation & filing of periodic TDS returns (24Q/26Q)

Tax Auditor Coordination

  • Compile and organize documents for tax auditor
  • Respond to audit queries
  • Provide corroborative working papers

🏛️ Indirect Tax (GST) Services

+

GSTR-1 (Outward Supplies)

  • Prepare outward supply details from accounting records
  • Reconcile with sales ledger
  • Prepare and file GSTR-1 on GST portal
  • Assist in rectifications if discrepancies arise

GSTR-3B (Summary Return)

  • Compile inward/outward supply data
  • Calculate net GST payable after ITC
  • Prepare supporting working papers
  • File GSTR-3B as per deadlines

Annual Return & Reconciliation

  • Prepare GSTR-9 annual return schedules
  • Reconciliation between GSTR-1/GSTR-3B and audited financials
  • Prepare GSTR-9C reconciliation statement
  • Coordinate CA certification and filing

💼 Accounting & ERP Services

+

Invoice & Revenue Management

  • Generate tax-compliant invoices
  • Ensure correct GST/HSN application
  • E-invoice issuance as required

ERP Entry & Maintenance

  • Record day-to-day transactions in agreed ERP
  • Post recurring journals, accruals, depreciation
  • Provide periodic reconciliations (bank, creditors, debtors)

Financial Statements & Year-end

  • Compile Trial Balance, P&L, Balance Sheet
  • Prepare schedules for quarterly & annual close
  • Prepare schedules required for audit
